Warlocks have always been the cool kids of the D&D world, with their eldritch blasts and pact magic. The latest update takes this to a whole new level, offering unparalleled customization options for your arcane warrior.

Whether you want to be a blade-wielding hexblade or a master of forbidden lore, the new Warlock subclasses have something for everyone. The power fantasy is real, and it feels fantastic.
