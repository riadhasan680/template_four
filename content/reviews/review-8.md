Finally, a Marvel game that gets it right. Marvel Rivals allows for destructible environments and team-up moves that feel ripped straight from the comics.

The roster is deep, and the balance is surprisingly good for launch. Whether you're swinging as Spider-Man or smashing as Hulk, every character feels distinct and powerful.
